    Andrea Piacquadio

    Nagarkot is a hilltop town located in the Kathmandu Valley of Nepal, about 28 kilometers from the capital city of Kathmandu. It is situated at an elevation of 2,175 meters above sea level and offers panoramic views of the Himalayan mountains, including Mount Everest on clear days. Nagarkot is a popular destination for tourists who come to enjoy the natural beauty and peaceful surroundings, as well as to experience Nepalese culture and hospitality. The town is known for its stunning sunrises and sunsets, as well as its hiking trails, temples, and traditional villages.
